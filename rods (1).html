Elevate Your Space with Custom Drapery Rods


Enhance the beauty and functionality of your home with our premium selection of drapery rods.

Whether you're designing a modern living room, a cozy bedroom, or a sophisticated office,

the right drapery hardware can make all the difference.


Types of Drapery Rods We Offer


1.Single Drapery Rods: Ideal for lightweight curtains and streamlined looks.

2.Double Drapery Rods: Perfect for layering sheer and blackout curtains.

3.Tension Rods: A non-invasive option for renters or temporary setups.

4.Motorized Rods: Bring convenience and luxury to your space with automated drapery solutions.


Finishes and Accessories


1.Finishes: Choose from a wide range of finishes, including polished nickel, brushed brass, matte black, and more.

2.Finials: Add a personal touch with decorative finials in classic, modern, or whimsical styles.

3.Rings & Clips: Complete your look with matching curtain rings and clips for seamless functionality.
